IS DATA EPHEMERAL?

About

A flower—an ephemeral existence, destined to fade. Yet, we, in our hubris, believe we can conquer time itself. We capture the moment, freezing it in a digital frame. But is this truly preservation, or merely a digital mausoleum?

In an era where information is no longer bound by physical constraints, our obsession with the tangible has shifted towards the intangible. A single click can capture a moment, a thought, or a piece of history into countless digital artifacts and preserve it in the realm of the digital.

Like a flower, a digital file blooms into existence, yet, beneath the illusion of permanence and the realm of ones and zeros, the march of time continues. Our digital data, once vibrant and alive, is susceptible to volatility. The once pristine image, the crisp audio recording, the eloquent text—all succumb to the relentless passage of time. Hardware fails, software becomes obsolete, and file formats disappear into the digital abyss.

IS  DATA  EPHEMERAL? delves into the darker side of the digital age, exposing the fragility of our digital heritage. It is a plea to future generations to remember the digital ghosts of the past and a critique of our obsession with the new, our disregard for the old, and our failure to recognize the impermanence of our digital creations.
